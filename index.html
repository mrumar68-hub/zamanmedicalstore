Welcome to Zaman Medical Store

Zaman Medical Store is a smart medicine inventory management system designed for pharmacies, clinics, and health professionals. It helps you easily add, track, and monitor your medicine stock with real-time updates. Whether you're keeping an eye on expiry dates or checking stock levels, Zaman Medical Store makes the process effortless and accurate.

  • ✔️ Add & update medicines with ease
  • ✔️ Auto-status detection (Low / In Stock)
  • ✔️ Expiry date tracking and alerts
  • ✔️ Clean and responsive design using Bootstrap
